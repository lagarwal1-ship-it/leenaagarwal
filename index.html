Why a TPM Consultant

What companies look for — and what I deliver

Research shows that the highest-performing AI/ML teams hire TPM consultants for six core reasons. Here is how I address every one of them.

🧭

Technical Depth + Program Leadership

Companies need TPMs who can evaluate architecture tradeoffs, navigate system design decisions, and lead engineering teams — not just coordinate. With an M.S. in Computer Science and 8 years as an engineering lead, I bring both.

🔗

Cross-Functional Stakeholder Alignment

The #1 reason programs fail is misalignment between research, engineering, product, and vendors. I build the operating mechanisms — exec reviews, dependency tracking, OKRs — that keep every stakeholder moving in the same direction.

⚠️

Risk Management and Go/No-Go Decisions

Companies hiring TPM consultants need someone who can own risk: identify it early, communicate it clearly, and make go/no-go calls with conviction. I have done this under NHTSA regulatory oversight, automotive safety constraints, and live-traffic deployments.

Data-Driven Execution and OKR Ownership

Strong TPM consultants do not just deliver projects — they own outcomes. I define success metrics, build dashboards to track them, run A/B testing programs, and connect every sprint to measurable business results.

🤝

Vendor and Partner Management

AI/ML programs increasingly depend on external data vendors, AI trainers, and cloud partners. I have managed multi-million dollar vendor relationships, negotiated SLAs, and built quality assurance frameworks for external pipelines at scale.

⚡

Speed to Impact Without a Full-Time Hire

Companies turn to TPM consultants when they need senior leadership immediately — without a six-month hiring cycle. I embed quickly, learn your stack and team dynamics fast, and start delivering value in the first two weeks.

My Process

How I work with your team

A structured, four-step engagement model designed to deliver impact from day one — whether you need a two-week sprint or a six-month embedded partnership.

1

Discovery and Diagnosis

I spend the first week embedding with your team, mapping the program landscape, identifying blockers, and diagnosing the root causes of execution gaps.

2

Operating Model Design

I design the governance structures, dependency tracking frameworks, OKRs, and escalation paths your program needs — tailored to your team size, stack, and velocity.

3

Embedded Execution

Handoff and Knowledge Transfer
